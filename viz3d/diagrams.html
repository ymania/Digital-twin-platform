Message Sequence

Shows the exact order of messages exchanged between components during one 5-second cycle, and separately the control command round-trip.

sequenceDiagram participant SIM as Simulator participant MQ as Mosquitto participant BRG as Bridge participant IDB as InfluxDB participant VIZ as viz3d participant GRF as Grafana participant USR as control.py rect rgb(13, 40, 24) Note over SIM,GRF: ── Every 5 seconds (sensor cycle) ── SIM->>MQ: PUBLISH room/temperature {"value": 24.5} SIM->>MQ: PUBLISH room/humidity {"value": 58.2} SIM->>MQ: PUBLISH room/fan_state {"value": "ON"} SIM->>MQ: PUBLISH room/fan_speed {"value": 1200} SIM->>MQ: PUBLISH room/light {"value": 320.0} SIM->>MQ: PUBLISH room/power {"value": 85.3} MQ-->>BRG: deliver room/# (6 messages) MQ-->>VIZ: deliver room/# via WebSocket BRG->>IDB: POST /api/v2/write (6 fields, 1 timestamp) IDB-->>BRG: 204 No Content VIZ->>VIZ: update 3D scene (fan spin, ambient colour, thermometer) end rect rgb(13, 20, 40) Note over GRF,IDB: ── Every 5 seconds (Grafana auto-refresh) ── GRF->>IDB: Flux query from(bucket:"room_sensors") |> range(start:-30m) IDB-->>GRF: tables [temperature_c, humidity_pct, fan_active, ...] GRF->>GRF: render panels end rect rgb(40, 13, 13) Note over USR,SIM: ── Manual control command (reverse path) ── USR->>MQ: PUBLISH room/fan/control "ON" MQ-->>SIM: deliver room/fan/control SIM->>SIM: fan_state = "ON"\nmanual_override_ticks = 12 Note right of SIM: auto-thermostat paused 60 s end
